About Us

The Intelligent Learning Experience Lab (ILXL) in the Computer Science Department at California State University Fullerton focuses on research that leverages technology to improve learning experience (LX). Our research is centered around students to understand their needs, reduce barriers to their success, and enable them to maximize their learning. We are also heavily invested in supporting instructors so they may engage in high-quality strategies that help their students learn effectively.
